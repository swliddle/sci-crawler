October 2024 general conference

October 2024 general conference

  • Saturday Morning Session

    • Saturday Morning Session

      The Saturday morning session of the 194th semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 5–6, 2024.

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

      President Eyring presents the General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers for a sustaining vote.

    • Neil L. Andersen

      The Triumph of Hope

      Elder Andersen teaches that when we have hope in Christ and put our trust in Him, we will feel of His peace.

    • Emily Belle Freeman

      Live Up to Your Privileges

      President Freeman encourages women and young women to learn how priesthood ordinances and covenant promises will allow God’s power into their lives, helping them to reach their potential.

    • Karl D. Hirst

      God’s Favourite

      Elder Hirst teaches us how to feel the divine love of Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ.

    • Dale G. Renlund

      “This Is My Gospel”—“This Is My Church”

      Elder Renlund teaches that the combination of the Savior’s gospel and the Savior’s Church will give us access to God’s power and help us become holy.

    • David P. Homer

      Trusting Our Father

      Elder Homer teaches that we receive blessings when we trust in God and follow His commandments.

    • Gregorio E. Casillas

      God Loves All His Children

      Elder Casillas teaches that we can bless the lives of God’s children through ministering and discipleship.

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      Following Christ

      President Oaks teaches the importance of following Jesus Christ’s commandments, with emphasis on His commandment to avoid contention.

  • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

      The Saturday afternoon session of the 194th semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 5–6, 2024.

    • D. Todd Christofferson

      Burying Our Weapons of Rebellion

      Elder Christofferson encourages us to bury any element of rebellion against God in our lives, whether it be actively disobeying His commandments or passively ignoring His will.

    • José A. Teixeira

      Bonded to Jesus Christ: Becoming the Salt of the Earth

      Elder Teixeira teaches four simple but profound ways we can remain bonded to Jesus Christ.

    • Juan Pablo Villar

      His Hand Ready to Help Us

      Elder Villar teaches that the Savior is always there to help us overcome any challenge if we have faith in Him.

    • Patrick Kearon

      Welcome to the Church of Joy

      Elder Kearon teaches about the joy that can be found in the Church of Jesus Christ.

    • David L. Buckner

      “Ye Are My Friends”

      Elder Buckner teaches that we must stop looking for reasons to divide and seek opportunities to “be one.”

    • D. Martin Goury

      Be Thou Clean

      Elder Goury teaches that daily repentance helps us be more sensitive and open to the guidance of the Lord and the promptings of the Holy Ghost.

    • Aroldo B. Cavalcante

      The Wind Did Never Cease to Blow

      Elder Cavalcante teaches that God can sustain us through our trials and that we can help one another on our spiritual journeys as well.

    • Ulisses Soares

      Aligning Our Will with His

      Elder Soares teaches that the ultimate test of our discipleship is found in our willingness to give up our old self and submit our heart and our whole soul to God so that His will becomes ours.

  • Saturday Evening Session

    • Saturday Evening Session

      The Saturday evening session of the 194th semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 5–6, 2024.

    • Gerrit W. Gong

      Holiness to the Lord in Everyday Life

      Elder Gong invites us to make holiness part of our everyday life, which will draw us closer to the Lord and each other.

    • Kristin M. Yee

      The Joy of Our Redemption

      Sister Yee teaches that through our covenant relationship with God, we can access the cleansing, healing, and redeeming power of Jesus Christ.

    • Kyle S. McKay

      The Man Who Communed with Jehovah

      Elder McKay gives his witness of the great life and legacy of the Prophet Joseph Smith.

    • Jorge M. Alvarado

      Embrace the Lord’s Gift of Repentance

      Elder Alvarado teaches about repentance and testifies of the healing available to all through the redemptive power of Jesus Christ.

    • David A. Bednar

      In the Space of Not Many Years

      Elder Bednar uses the examples of the Nephites and Lamanites in the Book of Mormon to warn against letting pride turn us away from the Lord.

  • Sunday Morning Session

    • Sunday Morning Session

      The Sunday morning session of the 194th semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 5–6, 2024.

    • Jeffrey R. Holland

      “I Am He”

      President Holland teaches about Christ’s complete obedience to His Father and His great love for each one of us.

    • Tracy Y. Browning

      Seeking Answers to Spiritual Questions

      Sister Browning teaches that God can help us grow spiritually as we ask sincere questions, obey His commandments, and place our trust in Him.

    • Brook P. Hales

      Mortality Works!

      Elder Hales teaches that because of the gospel of Jesus Christ and the plan of salvation, mortality works!

    • L. Todd Budge

      Seek Him with All Your Heart

      Bishop Budge speaks about the importance of quiet time to worship and commune with God.

    • Gary E. Stevenson

      Days Never to Be Forgotten

      Elder Stevenson looks ahead to the next 10 years as an unprecedented opportunity to share the glad tiding of the gospel of Jesus Christ with the world.

    • Bradley R. Wilcox

      O Youth of the Noble Birthright

      Speaking to youth, Brother Wilcox addresses the question, Why must Latter-day Saints live so differently from others?

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Simple Is the Doctrine of Jesus Christ

      President Eyring encourages us to teach the true doctrine of Jesus Christ simply. Then our loved ones will remember it when trials come, and we will be blessed.

  • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

      The Sunday afternoon session of the 194th semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 5–6, 2024.

    • Dieter F. Uchtdorf

      Nourish the Roots, and the Branches Will Grow

      Elder Uchtdorf teaches that the branches of our testimonies will grow as we nourish the roots: faith in our Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ.

    • Takashi Wada

      The Words of Christ and the Holy Ghost Will Lead Us to the Truth

      Elder Wada teaches how feasting on the words of Christ and listening to the Spirit will lead us to eternal life.

    • Ronald A. Rasband

      “Behold I Am the Light Which Ye Shall Hold Up”

      Elder Rasband teaches about sustaining the living prophet by following his teachings and example.

    • Quentin L. Cook

      Sacred Scriptures—the Foundations of Faith

      Elder Cook teaches about the importance of the scriptures, especially the Book of Mormon, in ongoing conversion.

    • Rubén V. Alliaud

      Sons and Daughters of God

      Elder Alliaud teaches that we are all the literal children of God.

    • I. Raymond Egbo

      Focus on Jesus Christ and His Gospel

      Elder Egbo teaches that when we focus on Jesus Christ and His gospel, we can feel joy even during trials and challenges.

    • Russell M. Nelson

      The Lord Jesus Christ Will Come Again

      President Nelson teaches that now is the time to make Jesus Christ the foundation of our lives and to prepare for His Second Coming.
