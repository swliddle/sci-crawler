April 2025 general conference

April 2025 general conference

  • Saturday Morning Session

    • Saturday Morning Session

      The Saturday morning session of the 195th annual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 56, 2025.

    • DallinH. Oaks

      Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

      President Oaks presents the General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for a sustaining vote.

    • JaredB. Larson

      Church Auditing Department Report, 2024

      Jared B. Larson reads the Church auditing report.

    • JeffreyR. Holland

      As a Little Child

      President Holland teaches the importance of becoming like a little child in order to enter the kingdom of God.

    • CamilleN. Johnson

      Spiritually Whole in Him

      President Johnson teaches that we can be spiritually whole as we wait for physical and emotional healing.

    • RonaldA. Rasband

      Right Before Our Eyes

      Noting that the Lord is hastening His work, Elder Rasband recounts the Churchs growth in members, missions, temples, and education efforts.

    • QuentinL. Cook

      The Atonement of Jesus Christ Provides the Ultimate Rescue

      Elder Cook uses the example of the handcart pioneers to teach that Jesus Christ will rescue us from the storms of life.

    • RicardoP. Gimnez

      My Love for the Savior Is My Why

      Elder Gimnez teaches about the importance of connecting our actions with our love for Jesus Christ.

    • HenryB. Eyring

      Draw Near unto Me

      President Eyring teaches that we can draw near to the Savior through praying, listening for guidance, and serving others.

  • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

      The Saturday afternoon session of the 195th annual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 56, 2025.

    • Neil L. Andersen

      Cherishing Life

      Elder Andersen teaches about the sanctity of life and the importance of nurturing and protecting it.

    • StevenJ. Lund

      Divine Authority, Sublime Young Men

      President Lund teaches about the duties of Aaronic Priesthood holders and the blessings of their service.

    • S.Mark Palmer

      Return unto Me That I May Heal You

      Elder Palmer teaches that it is never too late to return to Jesus Christ and His gospel.

    • Sandino Roman

      Faith: A Bond of Trust and Loyalty

      Elder Roman defines faith as trust in and loyalty to Jesus Christ and teaches how we can nurture and increase our faith.

    • DaleG. Renlund

      Personal Preparation to Meet the Savior

      Elder Renlund uses lessons from three of the Saviors parables to teach how we can prepare for the Second Coming.

    • Hans T. Boom

      True to the Faith That Our Parents Have Cherished

      Elder Boom teaches us to find strength in the faith and testimonies of our parents and ancestors.

    • DieterF. Uchtdorf

      By This All Will Know That You Are My Disciples

      Elder Uchtdorf teaches that our love for others shows our discipleship and that we can find belonging and unity in Christs Church.

  • Saturday Evening Session

    • Saturday Evening Session

      The Saturday evening session of the 195th annual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 56, 2025.

    • GaryE. Stevenson

      And We Talk of Christ

      Elder Stevenson teaches that as we accept the invitations from Church leaders to more intentionally celebrate Easter, we will find that our bond with Jesus Christ grows stronger.

    • AmyA. Wright

      Thou Art the Christ

      Sister Wright teaches about the importance of helping our children believe in Jesus Christ and become more like Him.

    • JamesR. Rasband

      The Plan of Mercy

      Elder Rasband teaches that God is merciful and the plan of salvation is a plan of mercy.

    • SergioR. Vargas

      Our Heavenly Guidance System

      Elder Vargas teaches that focusing on Jesus Christ will help us weather lifes storms and prepare us to return one day to our heavenly home.

    • D.Todd Christofferson

      Worship

      Elder D. Todd Christofferson teaches about the actions that constitute worship; attitudes related to worship; the exclusivity of our worship; and the need to emulate the Beings we worship.

  • Sunday Morning Session

    • Sunday Morning Session

      The Sunday morning session of the 195th annual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 56, 2025.

    • DavidA. Bednar

      The Times of Restitution of All Things

      Elder Bednar summarizes elements of the Restoration of Jesus Christs gospel and Church, including the First Vision, the Book of Mormon, and the priesthood.

    • StevenD. Shumway

      Participate to Prepare for Christs Return

      Elder Shumway teaches that callings and other ways of serving in the Church help us grow and prepare us to stand before the Savior.

    • TamaraW. Runia

      Your Repentance Doesnt Burden Jesus Christ; It Brightens His Joy

      Sister Runia teaches that our worth doesnt change when we make mistakes and that the Savior understands us and wants us to choose Him again and again.

    • Grald Causs

      Compensating Blessings

      Bishop Causs teaches that limitations and challenges will not prevent Gods children from receiving the Lords promised blessings.

    • GerritW. Gong

      Eternitys Great Gifts: Jesus Christs Atonement, Resurrection, Restoration

      Elder Gong teaches that Jesus Christ understands us perfectly in our grief and in our joy and provides us with the gifts of atonement, resurrection, and restoration.

    • JohnA. McCune

      Joy Through Covenant Discipleship

      Elder McCune teaches the joy we can find by making and keeping covenants with Heavenly Father and following Jesus Christ.

    • DallinH. Oaks

      Divine Helps for Mortality

      President Oaks describes various ways Heavenly Father helps us during our mortal journeys, as part of His plan for our eternal progress.

  • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

      The Sunday afternoon session of the 195th annual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 56, 2025.

    • Ulisses Soares

      Reverence for Sacred Things

      Elder Soares teaches that having reverence for sacred things will bring gratitude, revelation, and joy.

    • MichaelB. Strong

      Charitya Sign of True Discipleship

      Elder Strong teaches principles that can help us deepen our discipleship of Jesus Christ and become like Him.

    • ScottD. Whiting

      Beware the Second Temptation

      Elder Whiting teaches that we should resist the temptation to hide our sins and should instead seek divine help.

    • ChristopherH. Kim

      Harden Not Your Heart

      Elder Kim teaches that we can soften our hearts through repentance, humility, and trust in the Lord.

    • Patrick Kearon

      Receive His Gift

      Elder Kearon invites us to receive Heavenly Fathers gifts to us, especially the gift of His Son and the truth that we are Gods children.

    • Benjamin M.Z. Tai

      The Love of God

      Elder Tai teaches about how we can feel the love of God and Jesus Christ in our daily lives.

    • RussellM. Nelson

      Confidence in the Presence of God

      President Nelson teaches that we can have confidence in the presence of God as we fill our lives with charity and virtue.
