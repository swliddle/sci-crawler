  • Saturday Morning Session

    • Saturday Morning Session

      The Saturday Morning Session of the 194th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 6–7, 2024.

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

      President Oaks presents General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers for a sustaining vote.

    • Jared B. Larson

      Church Auditing Department Report, 2023

      Jared B. Larson reads the Church auditing report.

    • Jeffrey R. Holland

      Motions of a Hidden Fire

      President Holland teaches about the power of prayer and testifies that God answers every prayer.

    • J. Anette Dennis

      Put Ye On the Lord Jesus Christ

      Sister Dennis teaches about the importance, power, and blessings of making and keeping covenants with God.

    • Alexander Dushku

      Pillars and Rays

      Elder Dushku teaches us that spectacular spiritual experiences are rare and that the Lord typically gives us one ray of light at a time.

    • Ulisses Soares

      Covenant Confidence through Jesus Christ

      Elder Soares teaches about the importance of living by the covenants we’ve made, which will give us strength and confidence.

    • Jack N. Gerard

      Integrity: A Christlike Attribute

      Elder Gerard teaches that living a life of integrity means being true to God, to each other, and to our divine identity.

    • Henry B. Eyring

      All Will Be Well Because of Temple Covenants

      President Eyring teaches that as we make and keep covenants in the temple, we will receive many spiritual blessings, both now and in eternity.

  • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

      The Saturday Afternoon Session of the 194th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 6–7, 2024.

    • David A. Bednar

      “Be Still, and Know That I Am God”

      Elder Bednar teaches that when we are “still,” we can know that God is our Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ is our Savior.

    • Massimo De Feo

      Rise! He Calleth Thee

      Elder De Feo teaches that blessings come when we stay focused on Jesus Christ, continually repent, and allow ourselves to be guided by the voice of the Lord.

    • Brent H. Nielson

      A Record of What I Have Both Seen and Heard

      Elder Nielson describes the growth of the Church that he has seen throughout the world.

    • Jose L. Alonso

      Jesus Christ at the Center of Our Lives

      Elder Alonso testifies that as we place Jesus Christ at the center of our lives, we will find hope, strength, and healing.

    • Gerrit W. Gong

      All Things for Our Good

      Elder Gong offers reassurance that in our Heavenly Father’s plan, even tragedies and other difficult trials can be for our good.

    • Michael T. Nelson

      In Support of the Rising Generation

      Brother Nelson teaches that the relationships we have with the youth can influence them to make better choices.

    • Quentin L. Cook

      Be One with Christ

      Elder Cook teaches that we should strive to include others in our circle of oneness, that we are united by our faith in Jesus Christ, and that the essence of belonging is to be one with Christ.

  • Saturday Evening Session

    • Saturday Evening Session

      The Saturday Evening Session of the 194th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 6–7, 2024.

    • Shayne M. Bowen

      Miracles, Angels, and Priesthood Power

      Elder Bowen teaches that miracles have not ceased, angels are among us, and the heavens are open.

    • Steven R. Bangerter

      Foreordained to Serve

      Elder Bangerter teaches youth that they have been foreordained to fulfill certain missions in this life and that God can reveal these to them as they seek to know and do His will.

    • Andrea Muñoz Spannaus

      Faithful to the End

      Sister Spannaus teaches six ways we can prepare ourselves to face the world and be faithful to the end.

    • Matthew L. Carpenter

      Fruit That Remains

      Elder Carpenter teaches about the new and everlasting covenant of marriage and the eternal blessings it brings.

    • Dieter F. Uchtdorf

      A Higher Joy

      Elder Uchtdorf teaches that we can experience higher joy as we draw near to God, strive to follow Jesus Christ, and seek to bring joy to those around us.

  • Sunday Morning Session

    • Sunday Morning Session

      The Sunday Morning Session of the 194th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 6–7, 2024.

    • Ronald A. Rasband

      Words Matter

      Elder Rasband teaches that the Lord’s words, the prophets’ words, and our own words matter and that saying “Thank you,” “I am sorry,” and “I love you” can help us show regard for others.

    • Susan H. Porter

      Pray, He Is There

      President Porter teaches children about praying to know Heavenly Father is there, praying to grow to become like Him, and praying to show His love to others.

    • Dale G. Renlund

      The Powerful, Virtuous Cycle of the Doctrine of Christ

      Elder Renlund teaches that embracing the doctrine of Christ is not just a one-time event but a continuing process.

    • Paul B. Pieper

      Trust in the Lord

      Elder Pieper teaches that we can strengthen our relationship with God only as we choose to deepen our trust in Him.

    • Patrick Kearon

      God’s Intent Is to Bring You Home

      Elder Kearon teaches that God’s plan is designed to help His children return home to Him so we all can receive eternal life.

    • Brian K. Taylor

      Swallowed Up in the Joy of Christ

      Elder Taylor teaches principles to help us feel peace, hope, and joy during trials.

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      Covenants and Responsibilities

      President Oaks teaches about the significance of making covenants with God and the blessings that come from keeping those covenants.

  • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

      The Sunday Afternoon Session of the 194th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on April 6–7, 2024.

    • D. Todd Christofferson

      The Testimony of Jesus

      Elder Christofferson teaches what it means to be valiant in the testimony of Jesus and invites us to take steps now to be among those who are valiant.

    • Taylor G. Godoy

      Call, Don’t Fall

      Elder Godoy teaches that when we pray to our Heavenly Father, He will answer our prayers in a personalized way.

    • Gary E. Stevenson

      Bridging the Two Great Commandments

      Comparing the two great commandments to towers on a bridge, Elder Stevenson teaches the importance of loving God and others.

    • Mathias Held

      Opposition in All Things

      Elder Held teaches that opposition is necessary for eternal progression.

    • Neil L. Andersen

      Temples, Houses of the Lord Dotting the Earth

      Elder Andersen testifies that temples will preserve, protect, and prepare us for Jesus Christ’s return.

    • Mark L. Pace

      It Is Wisdom in the Lord That We Should Have the Book of Mormon

      President Pace teaches how studying the Book of Mormon brings us closer to Jesus Christ and blesses us in many ways.

    • Russell M. Nelson

      Rejoice in the Gift of Priesthood Keys

      President Nelson teaches how priesthood keys and temple worship can bless our lives.
