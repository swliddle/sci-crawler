  • Saturday Morning Session

    • Saturday Morning Session

      The Saturday morning session of the 195th Semiannual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 45, 2025.

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      Introduction

      President Oaks introduces this general conference, which takes place soon after the passing of President Russell M. Nelson and is therefore unusual but will proceed as planned.

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

      Elder Eyring presents the General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for a sustaining vote.

    • Gary E. Stevenson

      Blessed Are the Peacemakers

      Elder Stevenson teaches us to follow the Saviors admonition to be peacemakersin our hearts, at home, and in our communities.

    • Tracy Y. Browning

      Tune Your Heart to Jesus Christ: The Sacred Gift of Primary Music

      Sister Browning teaches that music can testify of the Savior and teach important gospel truths that can stay with us for a lifetime.

    • Ronald M. Barcellos

      The Lord Looketh on the Heart

      Elder Barcellos invites us to give our hearts to the Lord by committing to more intentionally follow Him.

    • Brik V. Eyre

      Know Who You Really Are

      Elder Eyre shares two ways to better understand what it means to be a child of God.

    • Kelly R. Johnson

      Be Reconciled to God

      Elder Johnson invites us to be reconciled to God through the Atonement of Jesus Christ.

    • Dieter F. Uchtdorf

      Do Your Part with All Your Heart

      Elder Uchtdorf teaches that we should work to develop the gifts God has given us so they can bless us and others.

  • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

      The Saturday afternoon session of the 195th Semiannual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 45, 2025.

    • Ronald A. Rasband

      The Family ProclamationWords from God

      Elder Rasband shares principles from the proclamation on the family and teaches that this document has a divine origin and should be treated with the reverence given to words from God.

    • Chad H Webb

      That All May Be Edified

      Brother Webb teaches how we can invite the Holy Ghost to teach us gospel truths.

    • Jeremy R. Jaggi

      Humble Souls at Altars Kneel

      Elder Jaggi teaches that we will be blessed as we keep the covenants we make with God at holy altars.

    • Kevin G. Brown

      The Eternal Gift of Testimony

      Elder Brown shares three truths that inform our testimonies and encourages us to choose to believe.

    • Gerrit W. Gong

      No One Sits Alone

      Elder Gong teaches that the Lord invites all of us to come unto Him and to be united with one another in His Church.

    • Michael Cziesla

      Simplicity in Christ

      Elder Cziesla teaches that applying the doctrine of Christ in a simplified way will bring us joy, give guidance in our callings, answer some of lifes most complex questions, and provide strength.

    • Quentin L. Cook

      The Lord Is Hastening His Work

      Elder Cook teaches that as the Lord hastens His work, Church members have a sacred duty to accept and welcome new and returning members. He offers counsel for these new or returning members.

  • Saturday Evening Session

    • Saturday Evening Session

      The Saturday evening session of the 195th Semiannual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 45, 2025.

    • Patrick Kearon

      Jesus Christ and Your New Beginning

      Elder Kearon testifies that Jesus Christ offers us new beginningsas many as we needwhen we are burdened by sin, weakness, or setbacks.

    • J. Anette Dennis

      Cheering Each Other On

      Sister Dennis teaches that we have a covenant obligation to love one another and strive for unity.

    • Steven C. Barlow

      Lovest Thou Me?

      Elder Barlow describes four ways we can show our love for God, which can help us recognize His love for us.

    • William K. Jackson

      Remembering the Sheep

      Elder Jackson teaches that we should focus on ministering to individuals in our service in the Church, following the principles of counting and accounting.

    • Neil L. Andersen

      The Atoning Love of Jesus Christ

      Elder Andersen teaches about the power of Jesus Christ to bring about forgiveness for those who have sinned and healing for those who have been wounded by the sins of others.

  • Sunday Morning Session

    • Sunday Morning Session

      The Sunday morning session of the 195th Semiannual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 45, 2025.

    • Jeffrey R. Holland

      And Now I See

      Elder Holland teaches that just as the Lord healed a blind man with a mixture of clay, He often uses humble or unusual things to bless and teach us.

    • James E. Evanson

      Go and Do Likewise

      Elder Evanson highlights service missionaries and teaches how their service helps bring people to Christ and sets an example for us.

    • Ulisses Soares

      Adorned with the Virtue of Temperance

      Elder Soares teaches that temperance harmonizes and strengthens other Christlike attributes. He invites us to follow the Saviors example and make this virtue part of our character.

    • Peter M. Johnson

      The Power of Ministering to the One

      Elder Johnson teaches that when we minister in ways that lead to the house of the Lord, we help one another become devoted disciples of Jesus Christ.

    • D. Todd Christofferson

      Look to God and Live

      Elder Christofferson teaches that looking to God brings blessings, including prosperity and the gift of the Holy Ghost.

    • Andrea Muoz Spannaus

      Prophets of God

      Sister Spannaus teaches how youth can gain their own testimonies of Gods living prophets.

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Proved and Strengthened in Christ

      Elder Eyring teaches that our challenges can strengthen us if we rely on the Savior and turn to Him.

  • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

      The Sunday afternoon session of the 195th Semiannual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints held on October 45, 2025.

    • David A. Bednar

      They Are Their Own Judges

      Elder Bednar explains how the Day of Judgment can be a pleasing, glorious day, rather than one we fear or dread.

    • B. Corey Cuvelier

      The Name by Which Ye Are Called

      Elder Cuvelier shares what it means to take the name of Christ upon ourselves.

    • Matthew S. Holland

      Forsake Not Your Own Mercy

      Elder Holland teaches us to follow the example of Jonah and invite Christs awe-inspiring mercy into our lives.

    • Carlos A. Godoy

      Smiling Faces and Grateful Hearts

      Elder Godoy teaches how the Saints in Africa live with joy and gratitude in spite of challenges by focusing on the Savior.

    • Dale G. Renlund

      Taking on the Name of Jesus Christ

      Elder Renlund teaches that when we take upon ourselves the name of Christ, we can become like Him.

    • John D. Amos

      The Good News Recipe

      Elder Amos teaches that we can find happiness as we do more of what invites Jesus Christ into our lives.

    • Ozani Farias

      The Book of Mormonan Immeasurable Treasure on Our Journey

      Elder Farias offers three suggestions for deepening our conversion to Jesus Christ through studying the Book of Mormon.

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      The Family-Centered Gospel of Jesus Christ

      President Oaks explains why family is so important in the gospel of Jesus Christ and describes things we can do to strengthen our families.
