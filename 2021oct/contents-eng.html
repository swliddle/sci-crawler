    November 2021

    • Highlights from the 191st Semiannual General Conference

      Provides an overview of some of the lessons taught during general conference.

    • Saturday Morning Session

      • Russell M. Nelson

        Pure Truth, Pure Doctrine, and Pure Revelation

        President Nelson welcomes people to conference and invites them to listen for pure truth, the pure doctrine of Christ, and pure revelation.

      • Jeffrey R. Holland

        The Greatest Possession

        Elder Holland teaches us to love God and follow Him completely.

      • Bonnie H. Cordon

        Come unto Christ and Don’t Come Alone

        Sister Cordon teaches that we are children of God and that our eternal purpose is to bring others to Christ.

      • Ulisses Soares

        The Savior’s Abiding Compassion

        Elder Soares teaches us that we should follow the Savior’s example of compassion for others by withholding judgment and having patience.

      • D. Todd Christofferson

        The Love of God

        Elder Christofferson teaches that the commandments represent God’s love for us and mark the path of healing, happiness, peace, and joy.

      • Clark G. Gilbert

        Becoming More in Christ: The Parable of the Slope

        Elder Gilbert teaches that regardless of our circumstances, the Lord can help us reach our ultimate potential.

      • Patricio M. Giuffra

        A Faithful Search Rewarded

        Elder Giuffra invites us to enjoy the blessings that come from having faith in Jesus Christ.

      • Dallin H. Oaks

        The Need for a Church

        President Oaks teaches the blessings of belonging to the Church of Jesus Christ.

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

      • Henry B. Eyring

        Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

        President Eyring presents the General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers for a sustaining vote.

      • David A. Bednar

        With the Power of God in Great Glory

        Elder Bednar teaches that honoring our covenants helps us receive the power of godliness in our lives.

      • Ciro Schmeil

        Faith to Act and Become

        Elder Schmeil teaches that we can become better disciples of Jesus Christ as we ask, act, and study.

      • Susan H. Porter

        God’s Love: The Most Joyous to the Soul

        Sister Porter teaches that Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ have a pure love for each of us and that sharing Their love can bless us.

      • Erich W. Kopischke

        Addressing Mental Health

        Elder Kopischke shares some observations about mental illness, based on some of the trials his own family has gone through.

      • Ronald A. Rasband

        The Things of My Soul

        Elder Rasband shares seven “things of his soul”—precious principles that give purpose to his life as a disciple of Jesus Christ.

      • Christoffel Golden

        Preparing for the Second Coming of Christ

        Elder Golden teaches that we are getting closer to the Second Coming, a mournful day for the wicked but a day of peace for the righteous.

      • Moisés Villanueva

        Favored of the Lord in All My Days

        Elder Villanueva uses the examples of the Savior, Nephi, and a young missionary to show how we can face adversity with joy and compassion.

      • Gary E. Stevenson

        Simply Beautiful—Beautifully Simple

        Elder Stevenson uses stories from four Latter-day Saints to illustrate ways we can fulfill our divinely appointed responsibilities.

    • Saturday Evening Session

      • M. Russell Ballard

        “Lovest Thou Me More Than These?”

        President Ballard teaches us how we can show that we love the Savior above all worldly things by believing in Him, serving Him, and serving others.

      • Sharon Eubank

        I Pray He’ll Use Us

        Sister Eubank reports on the Church’s recent humanitarian aid efforts.

      • Brent H. Nielson

        Is There No Balm in Gilead?

        Elder Nielson teaches that the Savior has the power to heal our hearts and sustain us through our trials as well as heal our bodies.

      • Arnulfo Valenzuela

        Deepening Our Conversion to Jesus Christ

        Elder Valenzuela teaches that we can deepen our conversion as we study the scriptures and learn more about Jesus Christ.

      • Bradley R. Wilcox

        Worthiness Is Not Flawlessness

        Brother Wilcox teaches that we do not need to be perfect to have the grace and atoning power of Jesus Christ in our lives.

      • Alfred Kyungu

        To Be a Follower of Christ

        Elder Kyungu teaches about four principles that can help us become better disciples of Jesus Christ.

      • Marcus B. Nash

        Hold Up Your Light

        Elder Nash teaches us to share the gospel in normal and natural ways so that we and those we share with can have joy and many other blessings.

      • Henry B. Eyring

        The Faith to Ask and Then to Act

        President Eyring teaches that we can receive revelation as we exercise faith and are willing to act.

    • Sunday Morning Session

      • Dieter F. Uchtdorf

        Daily Restoration

        Elder Uchtdorf teaches that we all drift spiritually at times but that we can get back on course by daily following the spiritual landmarks God has provided.

      • Camille N. Johnson

        Invite Christ to Author Your Story

        Sister Johnson teaches us how we can let the Savior be the author and finisher of our personal narrative by having more faith and letting God prevail in our lives.

      • Dale G. Renlund

        The Peace of Christ Abolishes Enmity

        Elder Renlund teaches that by putting the love of God and our discipleship of Jesus Christ first, we can overcome our differences and find peace.

      • Vaiangina Sikahema

        A House of Sequential Order

        Elder Sikahema teaches the blessings that can come when we live the gospel and do things in proper order.

      • Quentin L. Cook

        Personal Peace in Challenging Times

        Elder Cook shares five of Jesus Christ’s teachings that can help us lessen contention and find peace in today’s challenging times.

      • Russell M. Nelson

        The Temple and Your Spiritual Foundation

        President Nelson refers to work on the Salt Lake Temple foundation to teach how temple ordinances and covenants strengthen our spiritual foundation.

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

      • Gerrit W. Gong

        Trust Again

        Elder Gong teaches that trust is an act of faith and that as we trust in God and each other, we receive heaven’s blessings.

      • L. Todd Budge

        Giving Holiness to the Lord

        Bishop Budge reports on the Church’s recent humanitarian efforts and teaches that our sacrifices in these and other efforts are consecrated gifts to the Lord.

      • Anthony D. Perkins

        Remember Thy Suffering Saints, O Our God

        Elder Perkins shares four principles to help those who suffer find hope and joy in Jesus Christ.

      • Michael A. Dunn

        One Percent Better

        Elder Dunn teaches that each effort to repent, no matter how small it may seem, can bring great blessings.

      • Sean Douglas

        Facing Our Spiritual Hurricanes by Believing in Christ

        Elder Douglas teaches that we face adversity best by having faith in Jesus Christ and keeping His commandments.

      • Carlos G. Revillo Jr.

        Miracles of the Gospel of Jesus Christ

        Elder Revillo teaches that obeying the principles and ordinances of the gospel blesses us and helps us become converted.

      • Alvin F. Meredith III

        Look down the Road

        Elder Meredith uses the story of Peter walking on water to teach that if we focus on Christ and beware of distractions, we can be rescued.

      • Neil L. Andersen

        The Name of the Church Is Not Negotiable

        Elder Andersen teaches about the importance of using the revealed name of the Church.

      • Russell M. Nelson

        Make Time for the Lord

        President Nelson teaches about the importance of making time for the Lord each day and announces new temples.
