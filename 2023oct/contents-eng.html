October 2023 general conference

  • Saturday Morning Session

    • Saturday Morning Session

    • David A. Bednar

      In the Path of Their Duty

      Elder Bednar expresses his gratitude for Church members who serve throughout the world and are the strength of the Church.

    • Amy A. Wright

      Abide the Day in Christ

      Sister Wright teaches that with the help of Jesus Christ, we can all “abide the day” and meet and overcome the challenges we face.

    • Robert M. Daines

      Sir, We Would Like to See Jesus

      Elder Daines teaches principles to help us better see the Savior and understand God’s love.

    • Carlos A. Godoy

      For the Sake of Your Posterity

      Elder Godoy asks those who have fallen away or are not as faithful as they should be to turn back so they and their posterity can enjoy gospel blessings.

    • D. Todd Christofferson

      The Sealing Power

      Elder Christofferson teaches that the sealing power, which validates all priesthood ordinances and makes them binding both on earth and in heaven, is crucial for the gathering of Israel.

    • Ian S. Ardern

      Love Thy Neighbour

      Elder Ardern teaches that humanitarian efforts are ways to show compassion to others and to follow the Lord’s command to “love thy neighbour.”

    • Dallin H. Oaks

      Kingdoms of Glory

      President Oaks teaches about the kingdoms of glory after this life and the Church’s focus on helping us qualify for the highest degree of the celestial kingdom.

  • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Saturday Afternoon Session

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Sustaining of General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers

      President Eyring presents the General Authorities, Area Seventies, and General Officers for a sustaining vote.

    • Neil L. Andersen

      Tithing: Opening the Windows of Heaven

      Elder Andersen teaches that as we faithfully pay tithing, the Lord will open the windows of heaven to pour out blessings upon us.

    • Jan E. Newman

      Preserving the Voice of the Covenant People in the Rising Generation

      Brother Newman teaches that it is the responsibility of parents to teach their children to come unto Jesus Christ.

    • Joaquin E. Costa

      The Power of Jesus Christ in Our Lives Every Day

      Elder Costa teaches that we can find strength in our faith in Jesus Christ as we seek to come unto Him each and every day.

    • Gary E. Stevenson

      Promptings of the Spirit

      Elder Stevenson teaches the importance of seeking, recognizing, and acting on the promptings of the Holy Ghost.

    • Yoon Hwan Choi

      Do You Want to Be Happy?

      Elder Choi teaches that if we want to be happy, we should stay on the covenant path. The Lord will bless us and bear our burdens.

    • Alan T. Phillips

      God Knows and Loves You

      Elder Phillips teaches that we are children of God, that Jesus Christ redeems us and brings us relief, and that Heavenly Father is perfect and loving.

    • Ronald A. Rasband

      How Great Will Be Your Joy

      Elder Rasband teaches that to gather Israel, more missionaries are needed and invites seniors to take their knowledge and testimonies and go on missions.

  • Saturday Evening Session

    • Saturday Evening Session

    • Gary B. Sabin

      Hallmarks of Happiness

      Elder Sabin teaches five ways to find true happiness.

    • Joni L. Koch

      Humble to Accept and Follow

      Elder Koch teaches the importance of being humble and trusting in the Lord.

    • Tamara W. Runia

      Seeing God’s Family through the Overview Lens

      Sister Runia teaches that there is power and joy when we view ourselves and our loved ones from a big-picture perspective.

    • Ulisses Soares

      Brothers and Sisters in Christ

      Elder Soares teaches how to guard against prejudice and treat each other as brothers and sisters in Jesus Christ.

  • Sunday Morning Session

    • Sunday Morning Session

    • M. Russell Ballard

      Praise to the Man

      President Ballard testifies of the many blessings we enjoy because of the Prophet Joseph Smith, who restored the fulness of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

    • Emily Belle Freeman

      Walking in Covenant Relationship with Christ

      President Freeman compares walking the covenant path with walking the Jesus Trail in Israel.

    • Adilson de Paula Parrella

      Bearing Witness of Jesus Christ in Word and Actions

      Elder Parrella teaches that we can take the Savior’s name upon us by bearing testimony of Him through our words and actions.

    • Quentin L. Cook

      Be Peaceable Followers of Christ

      Elder Cook teaches that peaceable followers of Christ who keep His commandments will be blessed with peace as they face trials and can look forward to a bright future.

    • Dieter F. Uchtdorf

      The Prodigal and the Road That Leads Home

      Elder Uchtdorf teaches that it is never too late to repent and return to the path that leads to God.

    • W. Christopher Waddell

      More Than a Hero

      Bishop Waddell teaches that of all heroes, Jesus Christ is the greatest.

    • Henry B. Eyring

      Our Constant Companion

      President Eyring teaches that we need to strive to have the Holy Ghost as our constant companion.

  • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Sunday Afternoon Session

    • Dale G. Renlund

      Jesus Christ Is the Treasure

      Elder Renlund teaches that as we focus on Jesus Christ and stop looking beyond the mark, we will find the greatest treasures of the gospel.

    • John C. Pingree Jr.

      Eternal Truth

      Elder Pingree explains what truth is, why it is important, how we can find it, and how we should share it with others.

    • Valeri V. Cordón

      Divine Parenting Lessons

      Elder Cordón teaches that parents should teach their children the gospel culture and help guide them back to heaven.

    • J. Kimo Esplin

      The Savior’s Healing Power upon the Isles of the Sea

      Elder Esplin shares a story about a Japanese Latter-day Saint that demonstrates the power we can find in temple covenants.

    • Gerrit W. Gong

      Love Is Spoken Here

      Elder Gong teaches how we may use three languages of gospel love: warmth and reverence, service and sacrifice, and covenant belonging.

    • Christophe G. Giraud-Carrier

      We Are His Children

      Elder Giraud-Carrier teaches that we should remember that we are all God’s children and should love everyone, regardless of our differences.

    • Russell M. Nelson

      Think Celestial!

      President Nelson teaches the importance of having faith in Jesus Christ and making choices with the celestial kingdom in mind.
